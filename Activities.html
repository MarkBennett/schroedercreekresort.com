   Activities

  • FISH on Kootenay Lake: some of the best fishing in British Columbia (see Fishing)
  • Crystal clear water, great for swimming, boating, wakeboarding, skiing, kayaking, canoeing and windsurfing
  • Visit the nearby picturesque town of Kaslo (major grocery store, museum, shops, restaurants, artists, other services) (see Kaslo)
  • Go soak in Ainsworth Hot Springs: only 30 km away
  • GOLF at Kokanee Springs, Balfour and Kaslo Golf Club
  • ATV trails
  • Hiking trails
  • Kaslo Jazz fest
