   Campground Amenities

  • Beautiful treed sites
  • Easy access for larger RV units, with full hook-ups
  • Shady spacious tent sites
  • Sani dump for campers
  • Sheltered marina and boat ramp
  • On-site holiday trailers available for rent
  • Rental cabin (2 bedroom with full kitchen), available on a weekly basis
  • Sparkling clean washrooms
  • Showers
  • Store with grocery items, fishing lures, ice cream
  • Laundry facility and ice
  • Picnic tables, firewood and fire pits
  • Heated central meeting cabin for socializing (cards/coffee/visiting)
  • Fishing licenses and freezer facility for catch
  • Pets allowed on leash
